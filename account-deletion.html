Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: May 26, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Statewide Sports Hub account and the personal data associated with it. We provide two ways to do this: an in-app option (fastest) and an email request (for users who can no longer access the app).

Option 1: Delete your account from inside the app

This is the fastest method and removes your data immediately.

Step 1. Open the Statewide Sports Hub app on your device and sign in.
Step 2. Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation.
Step 3. Tap the Settings icon (top right corner).
Step 4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
Step 5. Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account and the data listed below are removed immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app or your account, send a deletion request to the address below. We will verify the request from the email address on file and process it within 30 days.

Email: statewidesportshub@gmail.com
Subject line: Account Deletion Request
Include in body:
  • The email address registered to your Statewide Sports Hub account
  • Your full name as shown on your profile (if known)
  • A short statement: "I request deletion of my account and personal data."

We will reply within 7 business days to confirm the request and again once the deletion is complete.

What gets deleted

The following data is permanently removed when your account is deleted:

Data category What it includes
Account credentials Email address, password hash, authentication tokens
Profile information Name, gender, profile photo, sports preferences, skill levels
User-generated content Posts, comments, direct messages, chat history, game/match notes
Activity data Match participation, scores, statistics, leaderboard entries, tournaments joined
Device data Push notification tokens, device identifiers tied to your account
Approximate location Any location data tied to your account or game check-ins

What is retained

A small set of data may be retained for legal, security, or operational reasons. None of this data identifies you personally after deletion:

  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics (e.g. "5 users played soccer this week") — cannot be traced back to you.
  • Server access logs — retained up to 90 days for security and fraud prevention, then automatically purged.
  • Backups — encrypted backups are rotated on a 30-day cycle. Account data in older backups is purged when the backup is rotated.
  • Records required by law — if any (none currently apply to a sports-organizing app), retained only for the legally required period.

Timeline

Method When data is deleted
In-app deletion Immediate — your account and primary data are removed from production databases as soon as you confirm. Backups containing residual data are rotated out within 30 days.
Email request Within 30 days of request verification. We confirm receipt within 7 business days and confirm completion by email.

Reactivation

Account deletion is permanent. We do not retain a "soft deleted" copy of your account. If you want to use Statewide Sports Hub again after deletion, you will need to create a new account from scratch.

Questions

For any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at statewidesportshub@gmail.com.
